Description

Tempest Trace is a competitive player-versus-player first-person running game whereby the player competes against the clock and against another player to get from the start to the end of a parkour/free running track while trying to keep their movements smooth and free flowing. The player is tasked with traversing an obstacle course in as short a time as possible while avoiding enemies who would try to slow down or eliminate the player.

Requirements
The game requires Windows 7 (or above) and .NET framework version 2.0 to run.
An Intel i5 2.4 GHz (or equivalent) and Nvidia 460 (or equivalent) are recommended.
Mouse and keyboard or game-pad are required to play. Both are required for split-screen mode.

Features

  • Immersive first-person running experience
    Feel the flow of your character through the world as you run, jump, climb, and slide your way around the world.
  • 2-player competitive local multiplayer
    Play with a friend in split-screen mode. One player uses the mouse and keyboard while another uses an Xbox 360 controller.
    Double the people, double the fun!
  • Context-dependend soundtrack
    Listen to the background music evolve over time as you progress through the game, setting just the right mood for your current situation.
  • Parkour-style environment-interactive motion
    Fully interactive environments allow you to move around the world with as much smoothness and flow as you can muster.
  • Multiple routes through each area of the game
    Apply your strategic mind to selection of the optimal route through the obstacle course. Has your opponent tried to take a shortcut? Respond by punishing them for their greed!
  • Use gadgets to foil your enemies or opponents
    You have a limited supply of tactical gadgets such as smoke bombs to throw off your opponents and gain the advantage. But use them wisely as you never know when you might need it later.
  • Dynamic user-interface that reacts to in-game movement
    The in-game user-interface dynamically shifts around the screen to give you an added sense of your character's motions.

How to play

To start the game, extract the download zip and run TempestTrace.exe in the extracted directory.

Player 1 Controls (Keyboard)

  • Look: Mouse
  • Move: WASD
  • Jump/Climb: Spacebar
  • Slide: Left Shift
  • Throw Smokebomb: F
  • Interact/Use: E

Player 2 Controls (XBox 360 Controller)

  • Look: Right Joystick
  • Move: Left Joystick
  • Jump/Climb: Left Bumper
  • Slide: Right Bumper
  • Throw Smokebomb: B
  • Interact/Use: A
